# Tech Stack

## 1. Mobile Development

*   **Programming Language:** Kotlin
    *   **Rationale:** Chosen for its modern features, conciseness, safety, and official support for Android development.
*   **UI Framework:** Jetpack Compose
    *   **Rationale:** Selected as the modern toolkit for building native Android UIs, providing a declarative approach that simplifies UI development and enhances maintainability.
*   **Background Processing & Battery Optimization:** WorkManager
    *   **Rationale:** WorkManager will be utilized for managing deferrable background tasks, ensuring that background operations are executed efficiently and do not lead to excessive battery drain, aligning with the project's optimization goals.
